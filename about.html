About Us

TechLearn is made by Grade 12 Students of STI College Ortigas - Cainta for their Practical Research 1 & 2, Investigation, Inquiries, and Immersion, and Entrepreneurship subjects.

TechLearn is about computer hardware literacy without any unnecessary, complicated, complex information that a normal person cannot understand. Which is why TechLearn is made to aid the complexity of computer hardware literacy in a short, descriptive, factual, and easy-to-understand way.
